Welcome to Akot Diocese

We are a vibrant community of believers rooted in the Anglican tradition, serving South Sudan with love, justice, and hope. Our diocese encompasses 10 archdeaconries, numerous deaneries, and parishes committed to spreading the Gospel and uplifting communities.
